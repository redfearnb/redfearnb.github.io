How I Think About AI Work

Systems First

PhD training means I treat every AI product as a system with feedback loops — not a feature. I design for the emergent behavior, not just the output.

Govern What You Ship

Governance isn't a blocker — it's what makes scale possible. Every production AI tool I ship has a documented risk profile, evaluation baseline, and escalation path.

Eval Before You Trust

I apply statistical rigor (95% CI, A/B benchmarks) to LLM evaluation. Vibes aren't a quality metric. Neither is "it looked good in the demo."

Build the Team's Velocity

AI-native leadership means every team member is a force multiplier — not just the PM. I invest in shared tooling, prompt standards, and AI literacy across the org.
